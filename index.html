Shippa

Shippa is a seamless interface to submit a request for shipping a vehicle from one city to another. Get an instant quote, and let the transporters come to you. Shippa is a web app inspired by a real life project idea. Similar to Uber in terms of deciding the cost of shipping based on the distance. Built with React.js and material ui on the front-end, and Node.js, Express, postgresql, websockets on the back-end.
